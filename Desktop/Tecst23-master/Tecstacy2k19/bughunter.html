Description


Have the capability to detect bugs in the code, starting from a semicolon!? Well, TECSTASY is a perfect arena to showcase your skills. BUG HUNTER is an event of TECSTASY 2k19 organized as a part of SUDHEE by IT department of CBIT. Bug Hunter is a debugging event in which you can discover the debugger in you. Don't wait until you have a bug to step through your code. Hunt the bugs in the given time and become the ultimate King or Queen of bug hunters. You can choose your interested language of programming to debug. Solve the set of questions given to you in a time of 20 min If writing code is an art, debugging is black magic.
